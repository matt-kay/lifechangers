ABOUT US

LIFECHANGERS IS A GROUP OF PASSIONATE, PROFESSIONAL THINKING CHRISTIANS, WILLING TO BE "BROTHERS' KEEPERS" AND PROMOTE INITIATIVES TO GIVE MANY A SECOND CHANCE.

Our Story

Life Changers Kingdom Ministry (LC) was one of 10 Kingdom Ministries set up in February 2011 by "Pastor Prof" Yemi Osinbajo.

When LC was set up, he brought together a group of passionate, professional thinking Christians, willing to be "brothers' keepers" and promote initiatives to give many a second chance. By the grace of God and under the counsel of the Kingdom Ministry coordinators (Pastor Onari Duke, Deacon Tony Arabome and Brother Chinedu Onyia), the Life Changers Ministry is aspiring to be a beacon of hope.

Our Mandate

To win souls to Christ and empower those in need, towards achieving self-sufficiency in education, vocation or enterprise

In doing this, continuous counselling, mentoring and monitoring are crucial aspects of our work. We arrange on-going counselling subsequent to initial counselling. We also operate informal mentoring, taking time to know our applicants and getting to know them better. It's not uncommon to refer an applicant to a house fellowship for spiritual counselling, nor is it uncommon to call applicants and find out how they are faring.

Become a Volunteer

Would you like to work as a volunteer at any of our events?. Yes. Here, you are welcome to our team and you can work as a volunteer.

Vocation
